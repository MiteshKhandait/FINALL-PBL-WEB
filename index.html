Impact of Online Education in India


As it has been apparent for the past several years online education has emerged as a powerful
contender for the next new education technology. Although the previous technological
breakthroughsthat have failed to live up to their initial promise, I strongly believe this time really is
different.
-Objective of the Study:
1) To understand the concept of online education.
2) To study current state and future of online education in India.
-How is online learning changing education?
Online education gives students an opportunity to plan their future course of action and fast track their careers .Through live and interactive digital learning, edutech firms are imparting comprehensive and specialized knowledge which will enable children and adults to learn with a purpose and instil a sense of belief in them. Such offerings are changing the way India learns by giving students an edge in learning, along with an opportunity to progress in their career

-Social media as a learning tool:
• Social media as a tool can be used to enhance elearning experience and make it more engaging, relevant and culturally diverse.
• Students can critique and share feedback on each other's assignments, work in collaboration to create content that can be easily accessed.
-CHALLENGES
1. Lack of Infrastructure and hardware facilities which hamper reliability of online learning.
2. Problem in finding willing skilled manpower to training illiterate rural areas of India.
3. No or very less computer based courses/skills taught to students in government primary schools to increase their knowledge about ICT importance in rural development.

Tips and Tricks to study Online

Well, online education has become a huge part of our daily routine, isn’t it? And we’re still confused about how to manage time and how to improve our productivity. In this part we shall focus on how we can achieve it as students. Studying efficiently and effectively will require you to:
• First get an overview of your syllabus and as you have got an Idea about it;
• Check out the study material available in your college and textbooks as well as ask your teachers about it;
• For the topics you face difficulty with, ask your teachers and if you face difficulty again, go to web and read about it on various websites(as this will definitely work making you clear your doubts as the more you read, the more you get to know the topic better);
• In addition to your regular studies, Do online courses related to the content your currently practicing (as this will help you to grasp the understanding the topic even more);
• Do small-small and good projects related to the topic, which will not only help you for a deeper understanding of the topic but also these projects can be added to your work in your resume.
